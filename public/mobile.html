WHAT WE ARE

Lovers of ancient history have found a home at Ancient Civilizations. This site was created with you in mind. Here you will find like-minded people discussing prehistoric societies, ancient cultures and medieval history. AC is about the history of the world, its cultures, and its civilizations.

A BREIF LOOK AT
ANCIENT CIVILIZATIONS

Our prehistoric ancestors left us tantalizing clues to their societies with megalithic structures such as Stone Henge in modern-day England, Göbekli Tepe in modern-day Turkey, and Tumshukayko in modern-day Peru. The first civilizations were in Mesopotamia, Egypt, and China. As human populations rose and hunters and gatherers settled, other civilizations sprang up around the world.

OUR GOAL

Our goal at Ancient Civilizations is to provide a platform for our members to share their knowledge of the civilizations most important to them. We hope that by doing so, they will provide information on global civilizations, their history and their culture.

As such, the site will include both general and specific details about the lives and places that made our ancient ancestors unique.
